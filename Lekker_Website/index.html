Lekker: Your All-Day Brunch Haven

Welcome to Lekker

Your all-day brunch haven where every morning starts with the comforting aroma of freshly baked pastries and the rich warmth of specialty coffee. Inspired by the cozy cafés and vibrant food culture of home, we’ve brought a little piece of the Netherlands to the heart of the UK. Our bakery wakes before the sun to craft fresh, flaky delights daily – from buttery croissants to traditional Dutch treats – all made by hand, with care. Pair that with our carefully selected coffee blends, brewed to perfection, and you’ve got the perfect start (or pause) to your day. At Lekker, we invite you to slow down, sip, savor, and feel right at home.

The Art of Brunch, Done Right

Simple Moments, Every Day

At Lekker, brunch isn’t just a meal — it’s an art form. Every plate is thoughtfully composed to be as visually stunning as it is delicious, with colour, texture, and balance at the heart of our presentation. Inspired by the elegance of European cafes and the cozy charm of Dutch hospitality, our dishes bring creativity to the table in every detail. Think golden poffertjes stacked with precision, vibrant smears of house-made compotes, and garnishes placed with intention – each dish designed to spark curiosity and delight before the first bite. We take pride in turning everyday ingredients into something special, where rustic meets refined, and casual feels elevated. At Lekker, we don’t just serve brunch – we serve beauty, flavour, and a little bit of Dutch magic on every plate.
